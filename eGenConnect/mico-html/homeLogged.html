About eGenConnect

eGenConnect, developed by TUT and UP students for the City of Tshwane Municipality, is a digital platform transforming embedded generator applications. By streamlining submissions and automating data checks, eGenConnect reduces delays and enhances accuracy, creating a faster, more accessible application experience for residents.

Our platform offers a user-friendly interface that simplifies the application process, making it easier for residents to apply for embedded generator installations. With integrated tracking features, users can monitor the status of their applications in real-time, ensuring transparency throughout the process.

eGenConnect also aims to educate users about the benefits of embedded generators, including their role in reducing energy costs and increasing energy independence. We provide resources and support to help users understand the technical requirements and compliance regulations necessary for successful installations.

Join us in revolutionizing the way residents connect with energy solutions and empower your community towards a sustainable future. With eGenConnect, efficiency, transparency, and community engagement are just a click away.
